Learn Galaxy

Curiosity-first learning — free courses, guides, and mini-projects to help students build skills and confidence.

Our mission

At Learn Galaxy we believe learning should be joyful, practical, and available to everyone. We build short, hands-on lessons and projects that help learners go from understanding concepts to building things they care about — whether it's a simple webpage, a math trick, or a robot prototype. Our focus is accessibility: low-cost tools, clear explanations, and a friendly community.

What we do

  • Free mini-courses and walkthroughs for students and beginners.
  • Project-first lessons that teach by building.
  • Community tips, study plans, and open resources you can reuse.

Core values

Practicality — learn by doing; every lesson ends with a small project.
Openness — content is free to access and reuse where possible.
Empathy — beginner-friendly explanations and patient teaching.
Curiosity — encourage questions, experiments, and exploration.

How you can help

Contribute a lesson, report an issue, or share Learn Galaxy with friends. We welcome contributors of all skill levels. If you're a student, teacher, or just someone who loves to build, there's a place for you here.
