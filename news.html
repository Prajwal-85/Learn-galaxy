Scientist Detect Biggest Blackhole Flare Ever Seen With the Power of 10 Trillion Suns

by Space.com

Astronomers have observed the most powerful black hole flare ever recorded — an event releasing energy equivalent to 10 trillion suns. This cosmic eruption provides new insights into how supermassive black holes interact with surrounding galaxies, reshaping our understanding of the universe.
